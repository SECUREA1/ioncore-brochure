Instructions for Building the Cooling System

  1. Design and Planning
    • Tasks: Create detailed blueprints and schematics.
    • Tools: CAD software, engineering team.
    • Deliverable: Finalized design documents.
  2. Sourcing Materials and Components
    • Tasks: Order all parts listed.
    • Tools: Supplier lists, purchasing system.
    • Deliverable: Receipt of all components.
  3. Preparing the Cooling Plates
    • Cut plates to 100mm x 150mm x 5mm.
    • Deburr all edges.
    • Tools: Metal cutters, deburring tools.
    • Deliverable: Ready cooling plates.
  4. Installing Cooling Plates
    • Apply thermal pads both sides.
    • Place plates between prismatic cells.
    • Tools: Thermal pads, hand tools.
    • Deliverable: Plates in position.
  5. Connecting Cooling Channels and Tubing
    • Cut tubing to fit system layout.
    • Connect tubing to plates, route to radiators and pumps.
    • Tools: Tubing cutters, fittings.
    • Deliverable: Functional tubing network.
  6. Installing Radiators
    • Secure with brackets to the frame.
    • Connect to tubing path.
    • Tools: Mounting brackets, hand tools.
    • Deliverable: Radiators mounted and linked.
  7. Installing Pumps
    • Mount pumps in frame.
    • Connect to radiator tubing.
    • Tools: Mounting brackets, hand tools.
    • Deliverable: Pumps in place.
  8. Installing Fans
    • Mount on radiators using screws.
    • Connect to power supply/control unit.
    • Tools: Screwdriver.
    • Deliverable: Fans ready to run.
  9. Filling and Testing the Cooling System
    • Fill with water/glycol coolant.
    • Inspect for leaks, verify performance under load.
    • Tools: Coolant, fill bottle, diagnostic tools.
    • Deliverable: Operational and leak-free cooling system.

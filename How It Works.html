InertiaCharge 50 - Portable Magnetic Inertia Generator

Harness clean, efficient energy on the go with the revolutionary InertiaCharge 50.

Product Overview

The InertiaCharge 50 is a portable, sustainable energy generator that utilizes magnetic inertia technology to convert kinetic energy into clean electrical power. Perfect for outdoor activities, off-grid use, or as an emergency backup, this innovative system delivers 50 W of continuous power and 200 Wh of energy storage in a compact and rugged package.

How It Works

InertiaCharge 50 uses a high-efficiency flywheel with magnetic bearings to store kinetic energy. When you activate the system, four copper-wound electromagnets accelerate the flywheel to high speeds, storing energy as rotational kinetic energy. Once at full speed, the system switches to generator mode, converting the rotational energy into stable electrical power.

Key Features

Magnetic Bearings

The magnetic bearings eliminate friction, allowing the rotor to spin with minimal energy loss, ensuring maximum efficiency and long operation times.

Electromagnet Motor/Generator

The four copper-wound electromagnets, combined with a precision control unit, generate both high torque for starting and efficient power generation once the rotor is spinning.

Advanced Cooling System

Integrated micro-pump and cooling fans keep the system within optimal temperature ranges, ensuring reliable operation even under high load conditions.

Technical Breakdown

Power Output

The InertiaCharge 50 provides a continuous output of 50 W and a total energy capacity of 200 Wh, enough to power smartphones, small tools, or emergency devices for several hours.

Battery & Energy Storage

Utilizing two 3.7V 10Ah prismatic Li-ion batteries, the system offers reliable energy storage with a Battery Management System (BMS) to ensure safe operation.

Dimensions & Weight

Compact and lightweight, the unit measures 150mm x 100mm x 50mm and weighs just 0.9 kg, making it highly portable and easy to carry on adventures or for emergency situations.

Magnetic Inertia Generator

High-Efficiency | Low Friction | Modular | 3000 RPM Stable Output

Overview

The Magnetic Inertia Generator (MIG-3000) is an advanced power generation system designed for high-efficiency operation at 3000 RPM. It utilizes magnetic field propulsion and inertia stabilization principles to provide reliable energy output with low friction and scalable architecture.

Core Features

  • Stable 3 kW output at 3000 RPM
  • 2:1 Helical Gear Reduction for Torque Management
  • Integrated Oil & Air Cooling Hybrid System
  • High-Efficiency Neodymium PMSG Generator
  • Flywheel-Based Energy Smoothing System
  • Real-Time Sensor Array with Microcontroller Logic

Detailed Specifications

Component Specification
Shaft 19.05 mm AISI 4340 Alloy Steel, Length 250 mm
Gear Ratio 2:1 Helical (50:25 Teeth)
Flywheel Radius 100 mm, Thickness 30 mm, Steel, 172.7 J Stored Energy
Generator 3-phase Neodymium Rotor PMSG @ 1500 RPM, 92-95% Efficiency
Cooling Dual: Oil Spraying @ 0.7 L/min + Air Radiator with 80 mm Fan
Sensors RPM, Temperature, Torque, Oil Flow, Vibration (MEMS)
Controller STM32/ESP32 MCU with PID Control & Emergency Shutdown
Power Output AC (120/240V via inverter), DC (12V/24V Rails), LiFePO4 Support

Modular Architecture

The MIG-3000 is stackable. Multiple units can synchronize across a DC bus to deliver up to 30 kW using 10 parallel modules. Control boards support dynamic load balancing and power routing.

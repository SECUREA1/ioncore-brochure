Permanent Magnet Synchronous Generator (PMSG)

Efficient. Reliable. Built for Renewable Power Generation.

What is a PMSG?

A Permanent Magnet Synchronous Generator (PMSG) is a high-efficiency electrical generator that uses permanent magnets to produce a rotating magnetic field. It does not require an external excitation source, making it ideal for modern renewable energy applications.

Key Features

  • High efficiency with minimal power losses
  • Maintenance-free permanent magnets
  • Compact and lightweight design
  • Direct drive capability (no gearbox needed)
  • High torque and power density
  • Reliable under harsh environmental conditions

Applications

  • Wind Turbines
  • Hydroelectric Power Plants
  • Electric Vehicles & Hybrid Systems
  • Marine and Aerospace Power Systems
  • Remote & Off-grid Power Generation

Working Principle

As the rotor equipped with permanent magnets rotates (driven by a turbine or motor), it creates a rotating magnetic field. This magnetic field induces an alternating current (AC) in the stator windings through electromagnetic induction, efficiently converting mechanical energy into electrical power.

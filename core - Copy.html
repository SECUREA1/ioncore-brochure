Subsystem Breakdown

  • Rotor & Flywheel
    200 mm Halbach disk, 40 mm 4140 shaft, dynamically balanced to ISO G2.5
  • Field Coils
    6 × 95 turns AWG-17, 1 mm gap → 1.2 T @ 10 A
  • Stator
    24-slot axial-flux litz-wire winding, Ld=30 µH
  • Active Magnetic Bearings
    2 × 6-pole radial (450 N each), 1 × 8-pole axial (2 kN)
  • Power Electronics
    SiC 6-pack, 800 V/600 A bidirectional; 2 × 350 A traction inverters
  • Energy Storage
    94 kWh NMC, 400 V; 150 F / 450 V super-caps
  • Control
    dSPACE MicroAutoBox III (1 ms), H2W Mag-Pilot DSP (20 kHz)

Power-Flow & Logic

Mechanical → Axial-flux → HV-DC bus → {
    • Traction Inverter → 3-phase drive
    • DC/DC 400→48 V → auxiliaries
    • DC/DC 400→12 V → LV loads
    • Bidirectional DC/DC ↔ Super-cap buffer
    • On-board Charger / V2X export
}
Mode Logic:
if (torque_request < torque_max) → motor mode;
elseif (battery_SOC < target_SOC && headroom_available) → gen mode;
else → idle-flywheel mode.

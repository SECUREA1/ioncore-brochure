How It Works

  1. Charging
    Solar/grid power charges both battery (chemically) and flywheel (by spinning it up).
  2. Power Delivery
    Battery supplies steady energy; flywheel supplies fast surges and backup.
  3. Smart Control
    System auto-manages which storage delivers energy for maximum lifespan and efficiency.

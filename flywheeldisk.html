How it works

1) Spin

A carbon‑composite flywheel rotor is magnetically levitated and accelerated by a brushless stator. Vacuum housing keeps drag ultra‑low.

2) Store

Kinetic energy is retained at high RPM. The Power Control PCB manages RPM, temperature, and safety while a supercap buffer smooths peaks.

3) Serve

On demand, the motor runs as a generator, feeding power to devices via Qi 2.0 magnetic wireless or USB‑C PD. Load leveling extends battery life.

FAQs

Is it safe indoors?

Yes. The rotor is sealed in a vacuum dome with multi‑layer containment and active overspeed protection.

Can I charge while using it?

Absolutely. The inverter manages input and output simultaneously with priority to device loads.

Does it replace my battery?

It complements batteries by handling spikes and reducing deep cycles—extending overall lifespan.

API access?

Pro and Edge expose RPM, temperature, SOC, and event logs over BLE/Wi‑Fi with a local web console.

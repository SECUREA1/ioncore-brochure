How it works

1) Snap

The Power Coin magnetically docks to the Holophone. Hall sensors auto‑align coils; a secure handshake authenticates the coin.

2) Power + Sync

Qi 2.0 inductive charging delivers up to 60W. At the same time, the coin’s encrypted NVMe storage mounts for instant AR assets and wallet access.

Launch holograms, beam product demos, and approve payments with a gesture. Remove the coin to hot‑swap—device stays live via supercap buffer.

FAQs

Is data on the coin private?

Yes. Storage is hardware‑encrypted and only mounts after a biometric handshake with your Holophone.

Can I use multiple coins?

Absolutely—swap coins throughout the day or dedicate one for work, one for media, one for travel. The phone stays live while swapping.

Does it work offline?

Yes. AR assets, tickets, and payment tokens can be stored on‑coin for offline use, then sync when you’re back online.

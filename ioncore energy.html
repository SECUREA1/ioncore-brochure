How It Works

Our Gen-1 unit fits in a 150 × 100 × 50 mm, sub-1 kg enclosure. A stainless-steel flywheel spins on magnetic bearings, storing kinetic energy. Dual-mode stator windings drive the flywheel as a motor and harvest energy as a generator. Onboard Li-ion cells buffer energy, while a boost-inverter chain provides 5 – 150 W of pure-sine AC or USB-PD output. A micro-cooling loop keeps coils below 60 °C under load.

Magnetic Bearings

Friction-free ceramic bearings with N52 preload magnets for zero mechanical wear.

Dual-Mode Stator

M19 laminated cores and 1 000-turn windings switch seamlessly between motor/generator modes.

Liquid Micro-Cooling

Compact pump, tubing & radiator keep stator temperatures under 60 °C at full load.

Integrated Inverter

Built-in DC-DC boost and 100 W pure-sine inverter or USB-PD provides versatile output.

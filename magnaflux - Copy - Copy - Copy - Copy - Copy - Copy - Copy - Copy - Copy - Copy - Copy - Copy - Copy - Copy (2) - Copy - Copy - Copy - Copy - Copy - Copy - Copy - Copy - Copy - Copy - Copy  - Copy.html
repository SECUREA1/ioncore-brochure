Energy Conversion and Storage

Regenerative Braking System

Our system captures energy during braking and stores it in a battery or capacitor for later use. The energy conversion is efficient, ensuring the system remains self-sustaining, reducing energy waste, and improving performance.

Energy Calculation

Energy stored = C × V², where C is capacitance and V is the voltage. This formula helps maximize energy efficiency and ensure minimal energy loss.

Energy Storage System

The energy storage unit utilizes lithium-ion battery cells arranged in a series-parallel configuration. This setup ensures the desired voltage and capacity for both small and large-scale systems.

  • Capacity: 200 kWh minimum
  • Battery Cells: Prismatic lithium-ion cells with integrated Battery Management System (BMS)
  • Cooling: Advanced cooling system for battery life preservation

How It Works

The system works by generating power via electromagnets, which interact with the magnetic bearings, providing a highly efficient mechanism to induce rotational movement. The system uses minimal mechanical friction, providing smooth operation with low maintenance costs.

The system captures energy during braking, storing excess energy for future use. The real-time sensors monitor the system’s performance, adjusting the magnetic field and ensuring optimal efficiency.

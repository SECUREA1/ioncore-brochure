Vision & Innovation

MagnaLuxe™ reimagines home lighting by harnessing magnetic inertia technology. Encased in tempered glass and handcrafted finishes, it transforms kinetic energy into a stable power source—freeing you from reliance on conventional grids.

Our mission: elevate sustainable elegance by uniting artisanal design with state-of-the-art engineering.

Key Features

  • Frictionless Rotation: 20 mm stainless-steel shaft supported by hybrid ceramic & active magnetic bearings—virtually eliminates friction at up to 3,000 RPM.
  • Precision Electromagnetics: Four N52 neodymium magnets (10×5 mm, 1.4 T) and a 500-turn coil deliver smooth 5 V DC power.
  • Intelligent Power Conditioning: High-efficiency Schottky rectifier & LM2596 buck converter maintain consistent voltage for sensitive electronics.
  • Smart Energy Storage: 3.7 V/2000 mAh Li-ion cell with integrated BMS provides up to 2.5 hours of continuous 3 W illumination.
  • Advanced Cooling & Monitoring: Liquid cooling, radiators, fans, and real-time sensor feedback ensure peak performance.

Vision & Innovation

MagnaLuxe™ marries artisanal craftsmanship with breakthrough magnetic inertia technology. Encased behind tempered glass, its sleek cabinet houses a precision rotor, rare-earth magnets, and advanced power management—all working in harmony to power your lamp, PC, TV, and fridge without a single grid connection.

Key Features

  • Frictionless Rotation: 20 mm stainless steel shaft with hybrid ceramic & active magnetic bearings for whisper-quiet operation up to 3,000 RPM.
  • Superior Electromagnetics: Four N52 neodymium magnets (10×5 mm, 1.4 T) paired with a 500-turn copper coil on a silicon-steel core.
  • Intelligent Power Conditioning: Integrated Schottky bridge rectifier & LM2596 buck converter for stable 5 V DC output.
  • Smart Battery Management: 3.7 V/2000 mAh Li-ion cell with overcharge/discharge protection, delivering ~2.5 h of continuous 3 W illumination.
  • Active Cooling & Monitoring: Liquid cooling blocks, radiators, fans, and sensor suite (voltage, current, temperature) for peak performance.

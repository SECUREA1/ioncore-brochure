Magnetic Inertia System

Efficient Energy Generation and Storage

Overview

The Magnetic Inertia System is a revolutionary energy platform designed to offer continuous, scalable, and eco-friendly power. Using magnetic bearings, dynamic electromagnets, and a smart control unit, the system minimizes friction, maximizes torque, and stores surplus power efficiently. It's ideal for healthcare, retail, residential, commercial, and even portable applications.

System Components

  • Magnetic Bearings: Frictionless support for high-speed rotation.
  • Electromagnets: Provide rotation, braking, and torque control.
  • Control Unit: Real-time feedback loop with sensors and microcontroller.
  • Cooling System: Actively regulates temperature with optimized thermal components.
  • Energy Storage: Lithium-ion based, from handheld to 200+ kWh large-scale systems.
